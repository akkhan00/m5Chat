About 5mChat

Created by Khalfan

5mChat is an innovative ephemeral messaging platform designed for privacy-conscious users who value temporary communications.

5-Minute Messages

All messages automatically disappear after 5 minutes, ensuring your conversations remain private.

Real-time Chat

Instant message delivery powered by WebSocket technology for seamless communication.

Room-based

Create or join chat rooms to organize conversations by topic or group.

Media Support

Share images and voice messages that also disappear after 5 minutes.

Responsive Design

Works perfectly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

Privacy First

No permanent message storage - your privacy is our priority.
